Starter Project: PowerZero

PowerZero is a tool that monitors electrical power while you're away, or in locations that aren’t always attended, making you aware of power events that might require attention.

A week ago, I had never touched or seen a Raspberry Pi. This is my first project and is well-suited for people as new to this as me. I created this as a starter project that could give me an end-to-end experience—something that wasn’t just a couple of LEDs and a button. I wanted a project that could be genuinely useful to someone while helping me learn the basics of Raspberry Pi, electronics, and programming.

Work in progress. Estimated completion Q2 2025.
